# SvelteKit Resources

## Knowledge

- [SvelteKit Docs](https://kit.svelte.dev/docs)
  Official reference. Use for: routing conventions, load functions, form actions, hooks, adapters, config. The single most authoritative source.

- [Svelte 5 Docs](https://svelte.dev/docs/svelte/overview)
  Covers Svelte 5 runes ($state, $derived, $effect, $props). Use for: anything about component reactivity, lifecycle, and the runes system.

- [Svelte 5 Migration Guide](https://svelte.dev/docs/svelte/v5-migration-guide)
  Use for: understanding what changed from Svelte 4 → 5, and why the runes model exists.

- [SvelteKit: Building Your App](https://kit.svelte.dev/docs/building-your-app)
  Use for: understanding the full data flow — load functions, form actions, hooks, and how they fit together.

- [Joy of Code — SvelteKit series (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@JoyofCodeDev)
  High-quality, up-to-date SvelteKit tutorials. Use for: practical walkthroughs that go beyond docs.

- [Huntabyte — SvelteKit videos (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@Huntabyte)
  Very focused SvelteKit content. Use for: form actions, auth, real-world patterns.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- [Svelte Discord](https://discord.gg/svelte)
  Official community. High signal, good for specific questions. The #sveltekit channel is active.

- [r/sveltejs](https://reddit.com/r/sveltejs)
  Active subreddit. Good for "is this the right approach?" and real-world pattern questions.

- [Svelte Society (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@SvelteSociety)
  Conference talks and community screencasts. Good for understanding ecosystem direction.

## Gaps

- No authoritative book yet (SvelteKit is too young). Lean on official docs + community.
